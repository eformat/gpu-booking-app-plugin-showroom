== Key Concepts Remember

=== GPU Resource Types

* **H200 Full GPU** (1.0 equivalent): Full GPU for large training jobs
* **MIG 3g.71gb** (0.5 equivalent): Medium workloads and inference
* **MIG 2g.35gb** (0.25 equivalent): Smaller training and inference
* **MIG 1g.18gb** (0.125 equivalent): Notebooks, development, experiments

=== Booking Priorities

1. Reserved bookings always win over consumed bookings
2. First-come-first-served for reserved-vs-reserved conflicts
3. Consumed bookings (Kueue) can be overridden but may trigger preemption
